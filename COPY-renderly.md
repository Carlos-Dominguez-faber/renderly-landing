## FAQ

### Q: How realistic does the virtual staging look?
**A:** Our AI generates photorealistic results that are virtually indistinguishable from physical staging in listing photos. The furniture has proper lighting, shadows, and perspective matched to your room.

### Q: Can I use these photos on MLS and listing sites?
**A:** Absolutely. Many MLS boards accept virtually staged photos as long as they're labeled as "virtually staged." Renderly adds a subtle watermark on the free plan; paid plans deliver clean, MLS-ready images.

### Q: How many styles can I try per room?
**A:** As many as you want. Upload once, restyle unlimited times. The free plan includes a set number of renders per month — upgrade for unlimited.

### Q: What if I already have furniture in the room?
**A:** Renderly works best with empty or mostly empty rooms. If the room has existing furniture, results may vary. We're working on a "remove and replace" feature for future releases.

### Q: Is there a contract or commitment?
**A:** None. Start free, upgrade when you're ready, cancel anytime. No long-term contracts.
